Edinburgh Castle
Edinburgh Castle is one of the most exciting historic sites in Western Europe. Set in the heart of Scotland's dynamic capital city, it is sure to capture your imagination. The scenery will take your breath away. Only 45 minute drive from Ninewells.
from ¬£19.50 per adult
Royal Yacht Britannia
Explore each of the five decks of The Royal Yacht Britannia and discover what life was like during Royal service on board Queen Elizabeth II's former floating palace. A great day out for all the family. Only 45 minute drive from Ninewells.
from ¬£18.50 per adult
Queensferry crossing
The 1.7 miles (2.7km) structure is the longest 3-tower, cable-stayed bridge in the world. The new crossing formed the centrepiece of a major upgrade to the cross-Forth links in Scotland and cost over ¬£1.3 billion. Its a great place to take pictures and see the view of the Firth of Forth. Only 50 minute drive from Ninewells.
free

Glentress is rightly celebrated for some of the best mountain bike trails in the country, but that's just the beginning of the story. Chocolate making at Cocoa Black
Learn how to make your own handmade chocolate truffles at The Chocolate & Pastry School in Peebles, only 20 minutes away. Developed by UK World Chocolate Master Ruth Hinks, Make Your Own Chocolate Truffles is a popular introductory class for those with little or no experience of working with chocolate. Participants take home 30-40 beautifully presented chocolate truffles.
from ¬£70 per person.
Hike in the Pentland Hills
The Regional Park has a stunning range of medium-sized hills stretching from the City of Edinburgh bypass down to Carlops. Three local authorities cover the Park, which is a mixture of publicly-owned land (such as Hillend, Bonaly, and Harlaw), and private land used for farming and sport shooting. Reservoirs dot the landscape, providing homes for many birds and mammals. Park to start your hike near Carlops, only 15 minutes away.
free
Fish on the River Tweed
Tweed is the second longest river in Scotland and takes great pride in the fact that it produces more salmon caught to the fly than any other river in Britain. The river is famous for its salmon run and attracts anglers from across the globe to come fishing on its banks. The closest locations are only 10 minutes from Ninewells.
from ¬£50 depending on fishery location
Retail Therapy in Edinburgh
Edinburgh city centre plays host to the biggest global brands as well as local boutiques. For rainy days the newly built St James Quarter offers a stunning gallery style shopping centre with a mix of designer and high-street brands as well as some world class dining experiences.
free
