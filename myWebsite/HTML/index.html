About Ninewells

Set amonst 8 acres of woodland this small countryside retreat lets you unwind and relax in the tranquility of nature. Each of our lodges offers a unique experiences with views across the woods and accompanying countryside.
Our location feels very rural and isolates but benefits from the villages of West Linton with golf course, pubs, restaurants and shop, only 4 miles way, the larger town of Biggar only 6 miles away and Peebles 12 miles. The journey to the capital Edinburgh is only a short 40 minute drive by car or there is a bus service that stops only a short walk from the site.
